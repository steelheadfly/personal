Granted Patent Application (EU & US)

I'm quite proud of my first patent application I filed while working for SSH back in 2014-2015. While messy, as I didn't really know the structural jargon at the time and only had a prototype to show for, it was granted in both EU and US. I still think the idea is well founded and worth pursuing one day. In short:

  1. VDOM tree represented as light-weight readable Abstract Syntax Tree (as a contrast to React's ambient and heavier VDOM objects)
  2. Device and use specific renderers as interpreters over the AST
  3. Instead of finding the difference between old and new VDOM, produce the difference as "transactions" using profunctor composition scheme (similar to Lenses/Prisms in Haskell)
  4. Send the difference as encoded messages through websockets to other clients
  5. Clients decode the message and apply a controlled AST merge
  6. Client specific interpreters choose how to repaint the scene as per the new AST
